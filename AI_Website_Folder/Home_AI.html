Artificial Intelligence:

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science developing systems that simulate human intelligence—learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and creativity. It processes vast datasets to identify patterns, automate tasks, and improve efficiency across industries like healthcare, finance, and marketing. Key types include Narrow AI (specific tasks) and Generative AI (creating new content).

Key Technolies of AI:
* Machine Learning- Systems learn from data and improve automatically.
* Natural Language Processing- Helps machines understand human language.
* Computer Vision- Enables machines to interpret images and videos.
* Robotics- AI used to control robots.
